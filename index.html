Our Story

It started with a broken wristwatch in 2013.
Neither of them knew it then, but that small, ordinary moment was quietly writing the beginning of something extraordinary.

⌚
🤵
The Groom
He Said
We were secondary school mates from 2010, but our paths never truly crossed until SS2 — and all because of a wristwatch I had lent to a mutual friend. When the watch got damaged, I heard she was terrified to face me. Apparently, I had a reputation for looking too serious. So when I arrived and found this innocent girl crying behind a classroom door, I was honestly just confused. "That moment stayed with me." I asked her to be my friend for just one year — to observe me and tell me honestly what people said about me was true. That one year quietly became twelve. After seven years of friendship, I knew. She wasn't just my best friend — she was the most genuine, peace-giving woman I had ever met. So I asked her out. She showed me real sege first. But I knew what I wanted, and I was not backing down. "The girl who cried over my broken wristwatch became the woman I cannot imagine life without."
👰
The Bride
She Said
My first encounter with Tee was in 2013. Someone gave me a wristwatch to hold — turns out it was his. Long story short, it got damaged, and I had to face the owner. I had already heard stories about him. "He no sabi talk," they said. So naturally, I disliked him from a safe distance — before we'd even properly met. But life said: No, you will face him. "I went expecting drama. He was calm. Confusion set in. 😂" We reconnected on 2go, started as friends, and somehow he became my personal maths tutor. I caught myself thinking, "Wait — is this really the same person I've been forming dislike for?" On my birthday in 2018, he shot his shot. I said no — I wasn't ready for any of that. He said, "No problem, I'll wait." And when I say he waited… he actually waited. Determination pro max. "On 31/12/2020, I said okay — let's try this thing. It has been beautiful ever since." All because of one innocent wristwatch that didn't survive. ⌚❤️
